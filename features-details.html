Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work on Mac and Windows?

Yes! Meta Injector Pro is a web-based tool optimized for all modern browsers, so it works seamlessly on Windows, macOS, and Linux without installation.

Will the keywords be accepted by Shutterstock/Adobe?

Absolutely. We format metadata according to standard IPTC regulations which are accepted by all major microstock agencies including Shutterstock, Adobe Stock, Getty Images, Pond5, and Alamy.

Is my data safe?

Your images are processed securely and deleted from our servers immediately after processing. We do not use your content to train our models.
